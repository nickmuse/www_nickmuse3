Originally a 2003 Yamaha TW200.
My dad and I made some modifications:

  • Cut down plastic guard supports on the frame
  • Fabricated a mudplate, & welded supporting tabs to the frame
  • Imported exhaust, seat, fender, headlight, tag mount, electric box
  • Aftermarket blinkers and tailight
  • Fabricated a rear light mount
  • Deleted airbox for cone filters
  • Lower Handlebars
  • Extended foot pegs
  • Upgraded jets
  • Stripped & powder coated frame & other parts
  • Stripped & painted tank
